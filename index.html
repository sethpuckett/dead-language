About

Sick of flashcards? Can't stay awake for your Spanish 101 podcast? Then Dead Language is just what you need! Who says learning a new language needs to be a chore? Dead Language combines fast-paced, zombie blasting mayhem and Spanish language review in one mind-melting edu-tainment package! It's loaded with games to help you practice vocab, spelling, conjugation, grammar, reading comprehension, and more! And all while dispensing righteous, gory justice to legions of undead abominations.

...That's the plan anyway. Dead Language is still in the early stages of development, but I'm adding new things every day. Check back for more updates!

I would love to get your feedback. What do you like? What don't you like? Is something confusing? Did you find a bug? Does the game crash on your system? I want to make this a fun study tool and I can't do that without your help. So please shoot me an email at seth@deadlanguage.io or reach out on Twitter with any comments you have.

Here are a few of the features I've got in the pipeline:

  • Additional levels with additional vocab
  • Power-ups and additional weapons
  • More types of zombies with different abilities
  • Music and sound effects
  • Additional minigames to practice grammar, reading comprehension, accent placement, etc.
  • User accounts to save progress & statistics
  • A 'smart' study system that will remember the vocab that gives you trouble
  • Options (font size, volume, enable/disable blood, etc.)

You can track my progress and see all the latest updates on the development log at log.deadlanguage.io.
