Sick of flashcards? Can't stay awake for your Spanish 101 podcast? Then Dead Language is just what you need! Who says learning a new language needs to be a chore? Dead Language combines fast-paced, zombie blasting mayhem and Spanish language review in one mind-melting edu-tainment package! It's loaded with thousands of vocab words across dozens of stages to help you build your vocabulary without falling asleep at the keyboard.

Dead Language is in the early stages of development. I'm still working out bugs and balance issues. More features and content are coming every day. Check back for updates.

I would love to get your feedback. What do you like? What don't you like? Is something confusing? Did you find a bug? Does the game crash on your system? I want to make this a fun study tool and I can't do that without your help. So please shoot me an email at seth@deadlanguage.io or reach out on Twitter with any comments you have.

Here are a few features still in the pipeline:

  • Additional game modes to practice accent placement, gendered nouns, verb conjugation, and Spanish to English translation
  • Challenge stages and never-ending mode for players looking to turn up the difficulty
  • Additional power-ups, weapons, and enemy types
  • Statistics to help players focus on the vocab that gives them trouble
  • Downloadable/offline mode
  • Achievements
